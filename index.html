Huiyang Xie is a freelance architect and visualizer based in Italy and China. He completed a B.Sc. in Architecture at the South China University of Technology, specializing in Historic Building Conservation, and an M.Sc. in Architecture at the IUAV University of Venice, with the thesis Carlo Scarpa: Foresteria Querini Stampalia, under the supervision of Professor Vitale Zanchettin.


He has gained professional experience in both architecture and visualization offices in China, Germany, Romania, and Italy. Currently, his focus is on 3D visualization as an instrument to build the unbuilt.
